Reverse QR

Ever wanted to send information or a file quickly from your phone to an untrusted device?

1 - Open this page on the untrusted device

2 - Use your source device (phone or tablet) to scan this code

3 - Submit the information in the resulting site

4 - Automatically and it will be displayed here
